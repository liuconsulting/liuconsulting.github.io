HARRY LIU

Harry Liu is the World Trade Center Orlando Asia Trade Representative. Having deep background affiliations and connections in the Asia Pacific region, Harry serves as an important contact and adviser for businesses, investors, and trade partners in Florida looking to access the Asia market. Harry works closely with government institutions and World Trade Centers throughout Asia with the common goal and cooperative efforts to further enhance partnership frameworks, and increase trade developments. Harry is bi-lingual in English and Mandarin Chinese and is a key consultant for Asia related trade inquiries and activities. World Trade Center Orlando provides assistance to various partnering organizations and entities seeking collaboration opportunities in the Asia Pacific region.
